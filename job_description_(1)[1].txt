
Job Title: Data Scientist

Location: Remote

Job Description:
We are looking for a skilled Data Scientist to help us analyze large volumes of data, generate insights, and build predictive models to support business decision-making. The ideal candidate should have strong experience in machine learning, data preprocessing, statistical modeling, and data visualization.

Responsibilities:
- Work with large, complex datasets to derive actionable insights.
- Build and deploy machine learning models for prediction and classification tasks.
- Perform data wrangling, cleaning, and preprocessing using Python or R.
- Create dashboards and reports using tools like Power BI, Tableau, or Matplotlib/Seaborn.
- Collaborate with engineering and business teams to integrate data science solutions.
- Present findings to stakeholders in a clear and concise manner.

Requirements:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Data Science, Computer Science, Statistics, or related field.
- Proficiency in Python (NumPy, Pandas, scikit-learn, TensorFlow/PyTorch).
- Experience with SQL and cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure).
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
- Excellent communication and teamwork abilities.

Preferred:
- Experience in NLP, time series analysis, or deep learning.
- Familiarity with big data tools like Spark or Hadoop.
